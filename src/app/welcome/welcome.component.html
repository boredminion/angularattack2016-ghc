Angle of Attack

In the distant future everybody has a spaceship. Fly around and shoot other ships and collect bounties or conduct peaceful mining and trade operations.

Movement

Your ship can move forward or turn. When a command is entered, there is a delay while your mighty engines turn the ship. If you issue another command, your previous order will be cancelled. To move forward press 'w' or 8 on your keypad (toggle your number lock). To turn press 'q' or 'e' or 7 or 9 on your keypad. You can also use the buttons on the screen.

Combat

If an enemy comes into range, fire away! It normally takes about ten hits to destroy an enemy but some ships have stronger hulls or weapons. Some weapons have longer range than others. To fire, press the spacebar.
